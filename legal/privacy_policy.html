Privacy Policy — Asset Tuner

Effective date: 2026-03-04
This Privacy Policy explains how Ivan Murzin (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Asset Tuner mobile application (the “App”).
Contact: ivanmurzin.developer@gmail.com

1) Quick summary

  • No ads: we do not display ads and we do not sell your personal information.
  • Manual-only: the App does not connect to your bank accounts or wallets automatically; you enter balances manually.
  • Core data: we store the asset tracking data you enter to provide login and sync across devices.
  • Analytics: we use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and improve reliability and features.
  • Subscriptions: purchases are handled by Apple/Google; we use RevenueCat to validate subscriptions and unlock paid features.
  • Account deletion: you can delete your account in the App (Settings → Profile → Delete account).
Asset Tuner is an informational tool for personal tracking only. It does not provide financial, investment, legal, or tax advice.

2) Information we collect

A. Information you provide

  • Account information: your email address (for authentication / email OTP), and an internal user identifier.
  • Asset-tracking content: the data you enter in the App, such as account names/types (e.g., Bank/Wallet/Cash), assets/currencies you track, balances (snapshots), and your base-currency preference.
  • Support communications: messages you send us (e.g., by email) and the information you choose to include.

B. Information collected automatically

  • Device & app info: device model, operating system version, app version, language, and time zone.
  • Identifiers: internal user IDs, session identifiers, and app-instance identifiers used by our infrastructure to provide authentication, sync, subscription validation, and analytics.
  • Diagnostics / log data: technical logs related to requests and error events (for example, to troubleshoot outages and bugs). These logs may include timestamps and device/app technical details.

C. Analytics data (Firebase Analytics)

We use Firebase Analytics (a Google service) to understand how the App is used and to improve performance and features. Firebase Analytics may collect or process information such as:

  • app interactions (for example: screen views, feature usage, button taps, and session duration);
  • device information (for example: model, OS version, app version, language);
  • approximate location information (for example: country/region) derived from network signals; and
  • identifiers such as an app-instance identifier and (depending on your device and configuration) advertising-related identifiers.

We use analytics for measurement and product improvement. We do not use your analytics data to show ads in the App.

D. Payments

We do not collect or store your full payment card details. Purchases and subscriptions are processed by the platform (Apple App Store and/or Google Play). We may receive limited information such as subscription status and transaction identifiers to provide paid features and prevent fraud.

3) How we use information

  • Provide the App: create and manage accounts, sync your asset-tracking data across devices, and show totals in your chosen base currency.
  • Operate and secure the service: authentication, abuse prevention, and maintaining system reliability.
  • Analytics and improvement: understand usage patterns, diagnose issues, and improve app performance and features.
  • Subscriptions: validate purchases and manage entitlements (paid vs free features).
  • Support: respond to your requests and troubleshoot issues.
  • Legal compliance: comply with applicable laws, regulations, and lawful requests.

4) Legal bases (EEA/UK)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, we rely on the following legal bases under applicable data protection laws:

  • Contract: to provide the App (authentication, syncing your data, and paid features if you subscribe).
  • Legitimate interests: to keep the service secure, prevent abuse, and improve reliability and user experience.
  • Consent (where required): for optional processing where your jurisdiction requires consent (for example, certain analytics choices).
  • Legal obligation: where we must keep or share information to comply with law.
Some jurisdictions may require consent for certain analytics. Where required, we will provide appropriate controls or notices.

6) Your choices and controls

  • Edit your data: you can update asset-tracking content inside the App.
  • Analytics controls: depending on your jurisdiction and device, you may be able to limit analytics and advertising identifiers using your device privacy settings. Where the App provides an analytics control, you can also use that setting.
  • Marketing: we do not send marketing emails by default. If we ever offer optional newsletters, you will be able to opt out.

7) Account deletion

You can delete your account from within the App: Settings → Profile → Delete account. When you request deletion, we delete or anonymize personal information associated with your account, except where we must retain certain information for legal obligations, security, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution.

If you have trouble deleting your account in the App, contact us at ivanmurzin.developer@gmail.com.

8) Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your data within a reasonable period, subject to the exceptions described above.

9) Security
