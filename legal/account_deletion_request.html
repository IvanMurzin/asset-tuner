How to delete your account and data

Asset Tuner allows you to delete your account and the data linked to it. The fastest way is to request deletion directly inside the app.

Option A — Delete from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open Asset Tuner.
  2. Go to Settings → Profile.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

This triggers deletion of your account and associated data from our backend systems used to provide login and sync.

Option B — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us at ivanmurzin.developer@gmail.com with the subject Account Deletion Request and include:

  • the email address used in Asset Tuner (or any details that help us locate your account); and
  • a short message requesting account deletion.
We may ask for additional information to verify account ownership before deleting data (to prevent unauthorized deletion).

What data is deleted vs. retained

Data that is deleted or anonymized

  • Account identifiers: your account record, internal user ID, and authentication profile.
  • Asset-tracking content: accounts/wallets you created, holdings, balances/snapshots, and related preferences (e.g., base currency).
  • User settings: app preferences stored with your account (where applicable).

Data that may be retained

  • Store purchase records: Apple/Google maintain their own purchase history. We may retain limited subscription/transaction identifiers needed for accounting, fraud prevention, and dispute handling where required.
  • Security and operational logs: we may retain certain technical logs for a limited period to protect the service and investigate abuse.
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics data is generally collected at an app/device level for measurement. It may be retained by Google according to Google/Firebase retention settings and may not always be removable on a per-user basis. We do not use analytics to show ads in Asset Tuner.
  • Legal obligations: where we are required by law to retain certain information for longer.

Deletion timelines (retention periods)

  • Processing time: deletion requests are typically completed within 30 days.
  • Backup systems: residual copies may persist in encrypted backups for a limited period and are removed as backups rotate.
  • Security logs: typically retained for up to 90 days unless a longer period is required for security investigations or legal reasons.
  • Purchase/transaction identifiers: may be retained as required for accounting, fraud prevention, and dispute resolution, and for any period mandated by law.

If you need confirmation of deletion, email us and we will respond after the request is completed.
