# Asset Tuner — Personas

**Last updated:** 2026-02-10

## Persona 1 — Digital Nomad “Alex”
- **Profile:** works remotely, moves between countries, has multiple bank accounts + cash.
- **Goals:**
  - know total savings in one currency (USD/EUR),
  - see changes month-to-month without doing spreadsheets,
  - avoid complicated accounting apps.
- **Pain points:**
  - money fragmented across countries,
  - exchange rates make totals hard to estimate,
  - manual spreadsheets get outdated quickly.
- **Primary use case:**
  - once a week/month: update balances; check global total before a big purchase or move.

## Persona 2 — Crypto Holder “Mira”
- **Profile:** holds multiple tokens across multiple wallets/exchanges; cares about quick overview.
- **Goals:**
  - see total crypto + fiat in one number,
  - track how holdings changed since last update,
  - understand which wallet/asset drives changes.
- **Pain points:**
  - multi-token wallets are hard to model in typical “account = currency” apps,
  - switching between apps/exchanges to approximate totals is tedious.
- **Primary use case:**
  - daily/weekly: check total in USD; update a few wallets after transfers.

## Persona 3 — Multi-currency Saver “Nadia”
- **Profile:** keeps savings in USD + EUR + local currency; not a crypto power user.
- **Goals:**
  - track savings growth over time,
  - keep things private and simple,
  - avoid bank connection complexity.
- **Pain points:**
  - conversions are confusing,
  - doesn’t want expense categories and budgeting.
- **Primary use case:**
  - monthly: enter snapshots for each account; review trend at a glance.
