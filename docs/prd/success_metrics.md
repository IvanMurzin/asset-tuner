# Asset Tuner — Success Metrics

**Last updated:** 2026-02-10

## North-star (MVP)
**Users can reliably answer “How much money do I have right now?”** in their base currency, quickly and repeatedly.

## Primary metrics (first 3 months)
1) **Weekly Active Users (WAU)**
   - Definition: unique signed-in users with ≥1 meaningful session in a week.
2) **Retention**
   - D7 retention: % of new users who return in 7 days.
   - D30 retention: % of new users who return in 30 days.
3) **Paid conversion**
   - Definition: % of active users who start a paid subscription.

## Supporting product metrics (recommended)
- **Activation rate**
  - % of new users who complete:
    - sign-in,
    - choose base currency,
    - create ≥1 account,
    - add ≥1 asset,
    - add ≥1 balance entry.
- **Coverage**
  - Median number of accounts per active user.
  - Median number of assets tracked per active user.
- **Freshness**
  - % of active users with at least one account updated in the last 30 days.
- **Rates health**
  - % of time hourly rate job succeeds.
  - Rate staleness distribution (age of latest rates).

## Monetization diagnostics
- % of users hitting free-tier limits (accounts/assets).
- Paywall view → start trial/purchase funnel conversion.
- Churn rate for paid subscribers (monthly).
